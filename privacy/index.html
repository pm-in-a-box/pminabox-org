Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

1. The short version

pm-in-a-box.org is a static website. It has no accounts, no ads, no analytics scripts, and it sets no cookies. We collect personal information only when you choose to send it to us — by email or through GitHub. The self-hosted PM in a Box software sends no telemetry to us; your PMS data stays on your infrastructure.

2. Who we are

The PM in a Box project is founded and stewarded by APM Help. You can reach us at taylor@apmhelp.com.

3. What this website collects

We don't collect anything directly. Two third parties are involved in serving the site:

  • GitHub Pages hosts this site. GitHub may log visitor IP addresses for security and operational purposes, as described in the GitHub Privacy Statement.
  • Google Fonts serves the site's typefaces. Your browser requests font files from Google's servers, which exposes your IP address and user agent to Google, as described in the Google Privacy Policy.

We do not receive, store, or have access to these logs.

4. Information you send us

  • Email. If you email us (for example, to join the alpha/beta or request a marketplace install), we receive your email address and whatever you include in the message. We use it to respond and to run the project, and we don't sell it or use it for advertising.
  • GitHub. Issues, pull requests, stars, and discussions on the project repository are public and governed by GitHub's own terms and privacy statement. Anything you post there is visible to everyone.

5. The self-hosted software

PM in a Box runs on your infrastructure. The software does not phone home: we receive no telemetry, usage data, or content from your deployment. Your leases, residents, financials, and operational data stay where you put them.

Marketplace listings you install — agents, skills, tools, and connectors — are third-party software. A connector to your PMS, email, or accounting system moves data between your deployment and that system under the third party's own terms and privacy policy. Review each listing before installing it; we do not receive the data that flows through your connectors.

6. Sharing

We don't sell personal information, and we don't share it except as described in this policy, with service providers acting on our behalf, or when required by law.

7. Retention and your rights

We keep correspondence only as long as we need it to run the project. You can ask us to delete emails you've sent us, or ask what we hold about you, by writing to taylor@apmhelp.com. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, deletion, or portability) under laws like the GDPR or the CCPA — we'll honor valid requests.

8. Children

The Services are for property-management professionals and are not directed to children under 16. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page with an updated effective date, and material changes will be noted in the project repository.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: taylor@apmhelp.com. See also our End User License Agreement.
