Connectors

Outside systems and data, available inside the box.

Connectors are the integration layer, often MCPs. They connect property management software, maintenance systems, leasing automation, email, calendars, payments, accounting, and other data sources to approved agents and skills.

Default ranking: installs first, then active usage and operator retention.
