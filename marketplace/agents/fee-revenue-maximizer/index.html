What it does

  1. Indexes owner agreements, lease fee terms, ledgers, company fee policy, and recent owner communication.
  2. Flags potential missed fees, stale settings, inconsistent enforcement, or agreement-policy mismatches.
  3. Builds an evidence pack with source documents, affected accounts, estimated impact, and risk notes.
  4. Drafts recommended next steps for internal review before any owner or resident communication goes out.
