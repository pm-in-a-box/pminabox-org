Tools

Reusable actions skills and agents can call.

Tools are broad capabilities rather than product-specific data access. They help agents read, transform, search, draft, approve, and act across PM workflows.

DocumentsTool

PDF Extractor and Viewer

Extracts structured text, tables, clauses, and evidence snippets from leases, agreements, invoices, and notices.

Used by
Agreement review, income verification, compliance skills
DataTool

Spreadsheet Manipulator

Reads, cleans, transforms, and writes workbook data for rent rolls, ledgers, reports, and reconciliation files.

Used by
Accounting, comps, revenue, reporting agents
WebTool

Browser Automation

Navigates approved web apps, gathers screen evidence, and completes repeatable workflows with human approval.

Used by
Listing, research, vendor portal, and data entry skills
GovernanceTool

Approval Workflow Runner

Routes suggested actions to the right person before sending messages, charging fees, changing records, or contacting vendors.

Used by
Most externally acting agents
CommsTool

Message Composer

Drafts email, SMS, and chat responses from approved templates, tone rules, account history, and agent context.

Used by
Leasing, maintenance, collections, owner reporting
DocumentsTool

Redaction and Evidence Pack Builder

Creates review packets with sensitive fields redacted and source evidence preserved for auditability.

Used by
Compliance, disputes, screening, accounting review
